Our Story: From 6th Street to the Skyline

In 1998, in a small workshop on 6th Street, Oz Steel Inc. was founded with a simple goal: to provide uncompromising quality and reliability to New York's builders. What began with a handful of contracts in Brooklyn quickly grew through word-of-mouth, as our reputation for precision fabrication and honest partnership spread throughout the five boroughs.

Today, while our projects touch the sky, our feet remain firmly planted in the same Brooklyn community where it all began. We are proud of our history and excited for the future we are helping to build.
